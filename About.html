ARC Research Club

Mission Statement

About US: Any undergraduate student interested in pursuing graduate school should be critically aware of the research going on in their chosen field. We at the ARC Reseach club hope to educate students on the practicalities and methods of research as well as get our hands dirty with some first-hand projects of our own. With a top notch staff of American River College Professors that have experience in these fields of research, this student run club is a great way for you to experience what may be in store for your academic or career goals
